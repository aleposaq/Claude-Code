HOW TO PLAY

Guide light from every source into every crystal, matching its exact colour. Tap a cell to drop a mirror ╱, tap again to flip it ╲, once more to remove it. Beat par for 3 stars.

◉
Source — fires a beam in its arrow direction.
◆
Crystal — light it with its exact colour. Stray light ruins it.
╱
Mirror — you place these; bends the beam 90°.
▦
Wall — blocks light. (A faint mirror is a fixed mirror — immovable.)
▢
Gel — tints white to R/G/B. The wrong colour is absorbed — the beam dies. You can't re-tint.
▨
Splitter — turns one beam into two.
▲
Prism — splits white: red left, green straight, blue right.
✸
Bleach — washes a beam back to white.
⟳
Rotator — cycles a colour: red→green→blue→red.
⊕
Tinter — adds a colour to the beam (its glow tells you which).
▥
Gate — a locked door; opens only for its exact colour.
◍
Portal — teleports a beam to its matching ring.
✦
Void — a black hole. If any beam touches it, you lose. Keep it clean.

Mixing: a crystal adds up all light it receives. red+blue=magenta, red+green=yellow, green+blue=cyan, all three = white. A stray ray ruins the mix.

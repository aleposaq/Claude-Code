HOW TO PLAY

Guide light from each source into every crystal, matching its colour. Tap a cell to drop a mirror ╱, tap again to flip it ╲, once more to remove it.

◉
Source — fires a white beam.
◆
Crystal — light it with its exact colour.
╱
Mirror — you place these; bends the beam 90°.
▦
Wall — blocks light.
▣
Gel — tints white to R/G/B. The wrong colour is absorbed — the beam dies. You can't re-tint.
◇
Splitter — turns one beam into two.
✦
Bleach — washes a beam back to white.
▲
Prism — splits white: red bends left, green goes straight, blue bends right.
▯
Gate — a coloured door. Opens only for a beam of its exact colour; anything else is blocked.
◎
Portal — a wormhole. A beam entering one mouth leaves its twin, still heading the same way.
✷
Void — the abyss. If ANY beam touches it, the whole run fails. Let nothing stray in.

Mixing: a crystal adds up all light it receives. red+blue=purple, red+green=yellow, green+blue=cyan, all three = white. A stray ray ruins the mix.
